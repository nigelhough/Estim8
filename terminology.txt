### A summary of Project Terminology

EstimationSession = A session for estimating many stories

StoryEstimation = Estimating a single story

Voting Round = A round of voting during story estimation

Vote = A estimate fora story

Story = A user story to be estimated

StoryEstimate = The final story estimate

User = A user that will vote on a stories weight

Administrator / Master = A user that will control the voting Estimation Session
